## Account Management

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="How do you create or update your account?">
    Follow these steps to set up or modify your account.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
        Visit the dashboard and click "Create Account". Enter your email and a strong password.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
        Check your inbox for the verification link from `no-reply@learn-docs.com`.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Update Profile" icon="edit-3">
        Go to Settings > Profile. Update your name, avatar, and preferences.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Callout kind="tip">
      Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) immediately after signup for added security.
    </Callout>
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="What if you forget your password?">
    Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email to receive a reset link valid for 15 minutes. Change to a password with at least 12 characters, including uppercase, numbers, and symbols.
  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>

## Document Sharing

<Expandable title="How do you share documents with your team?">
  Generate shareable links or invite collaborators directly.

  <Tabs>
    <Tab title="Public Link" icon="link">
      In the document editor, click Share > Generate Link. Set permissions to "View" or "Edit". Copy the URL like `https://learn-docs.com/project/docs/abc123`.
    </Tab>
    <Tab title="Team Invite" icon="users">
      Go to Share > Invite Members. Enter emails and select roles: Viewer, Editor, or Admin.
    </Tab>
  </Tabs>

  <CodeGroup tabs="JavaScript,cURL">
  ```javascript
  // Embed doc in your app
  const embedUrl = 'https://learn-docs.com/embed/abc123';
  iframe.src = embedUrl;
  ```
  ```bash
  # API to share doc
  curl -X POST https://api.learn-docs.com/v1/docs/abc123/share \
    -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_TOKEN" \
    -d '{"email": "user@example.com", "role": "editor"}'
  ```
  </CodeGroup>
</Expandable>

## Mobile Access

Access your documentation seamlessly on mobile devices.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="iOS" icon="apple">
    Download the learn app from the App Store. Log in with your credentials. Sync happens automatically over Wi-Fi.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Android" icon="android">
    Install from Google Play. Enable notifications for updates. Use offline mode for cached docs.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout kind="info">
  Mobile apps support full editing. For best performance, keep your app updated to the latest version.
</Callout>

## Data Security

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="How does learn protect your data?" default-open="true">
    learn uses AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit. Regular security audits occur quarterly. You control access with granular permissions.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="What steps do you take to secure your account?">
    <ol>
      <li>Use strong, unique passwords.</li>
      <li>Enable 2FA in Settings > Security.</li>
      <li>Review active sessions and revoke suspicious ones.</li>
      <li>Avoid sharing `YOUR_API_KEY` publicly.</li>
    </ol>

    <Callout kind="danger">
      Never commit secrets to public repositories. Use environment variables instead.
    </Callout>
  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>
